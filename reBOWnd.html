reBOWnd

  • Year: Sophomore
  • Project URL: Link to GitHub

reBOWned is a a strategy game where you only have three shots to take out all of the enemies in each of the six levels. This entire game was created under 24 hours with the help of two friends as a submission for HACKSTL, a hackathon at my local library. The arrows can be fired from the bow at any angle as it travels for a couple seconds, bouncing off the border and terrain, and pierces enemies only to vanish after a couple seconds. The goal is to construct a perfect path that kills every enemy in one shot of the bow. Every arrow used past the first one results in one less star on the victory board until all three are used and it results in a game over. Each level was handcrafted to ramp up in difficulty as well as utilize the unique bounce mechanic to create an enjoyable experience.
